Your life will be much easier

extension

Collection

After setting up your account and your desired protocols our team will answer the calls you can't. The clients will be greeted and information collected on the circumstances and status of the animal. If it is after hours and an emergency our staff will refer that client to the emergency clinic of your choosing. If it isn’t an Emergency all the information will be collected, and the following morning your practice will receive an email with all the information so you can follow up with the client.

Interpretation

After you have received your email with all the information regarding your client you and your staff can overlook the case and pick the best course of action. The email will include all the symptoms of the patient, the type of animal, as well as the voice recording of the call in case you want to review the information more thoroughly. This will allow you to properly diagnose what you believe to be the issue.

Action

When you and your team have discussed the best course of action you can reach out to your client and book an appointment. If you need to reach out to the owner to get more information you can do that as well..
