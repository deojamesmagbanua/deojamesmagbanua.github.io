JEMA GAMER

- Branding

Overview

This logo is for Jema Gamer, a young fashion designer from Manila, PH. She specializes in luxury clothing (i.e. gowns, coat, and custom made garments.) Her design aesthetic are androgynous, strong, and sophisticated.

The logo is a minimalist typography with a twist of misalignment of her first and last name to represent the significantly strong distrinction of her brand. A simple typeface to give a straightforward feeling and a forward slash "/" to give that forward thinking on an edgier approach.
